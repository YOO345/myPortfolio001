PERFORMANCE

MAKE YOUR MOVE

Excitement knows no bounds in this extreme sports sedan.
BMW M engineering levels up every drive with unmatched agility and power.

More power to you.

The incredible 3.0-liter BMW M TwinPower Turbo inline 6-cylinder engine outperforms your competitors with a 0-60 mph acceleration time of just 3.8 seconds and a staggering 503 horsepower in the Competition model.

Transmissions for all.

A manual transmission comes standard for the performance-obsessed, while the standard 8-speed M STEPTRONIC Transmission in the M3 Competition model offers instantaneous and precise shifts.

Dynamics for the next personal best.

Achieve next-level agility, traction, and handling on the track or during your daily commute with the standard M Sport Differential and Adaptive M Suspension.

Bold brakes.

Standard M Compound brakes with red, blue, or black calipers provide exceptional stopping power. Choose the available M Carbon Ceramic brakes with gold calipers for high-performance driving and a high-profile look.
